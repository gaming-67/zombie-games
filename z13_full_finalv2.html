How to Play

WASD to move. Mouse to look. Left click to shoot. R reload. 1–6 switch weapons. F toggle flashlight (battery drains). E to pick up / open. T to interact with survivors/traders. V melee, G grenade, M toggle big map, Esc to pause.

Infection increases when bitten — use antivirals to lower it. Complete objectives to progress chapters. Save/load available in menu.
