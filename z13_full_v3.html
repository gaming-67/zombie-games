How to Play — Z-13: The Quarantine

Welcome to Z-13. Survive waves of infected, scavenge supplies, and use strategy to hold out.

Basic Controls

  • WASD — Move
  • Mouse — Look / Aim
  • Left Click — Shoot
  • R — Reload
  • E — Pick up items
  • G — Use grenade
  • Esc — Pause / Menu

Objectives & Tips

  • Clear the required number of zombies each wave to progress.
  • Loot ammo and medkits to stay stocked — pickups appear from slain enemies and crates.
  • Use cover: cars and crates reduce exposure to hordes.
  • Higher waves increase enemy numbers and toughness — conserve ammo and craft a plan.
