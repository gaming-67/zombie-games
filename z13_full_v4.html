How to Play — Z-13: The Quarantine

Survive waves of infected, scavenge supplies, and hold out until extraction. The game is realistic in lighting — use your flashlight and cover carefully.

Controls

  • WASD — Move
  • Mouse — Look / Aim
  • Left Click — Shoot
  • R — Reload
  • E — Pick up items
  • G — Use grenade
  • F — Toggle flashlight (uses battery)
  • Esc — Pause

Tips

  • Loot ammo and medkits from corpses and crates.
  • Armor reduces damage — look for armor plates.
  • Higher waves spawn stronger and closer enemies.
  • At wave 20, extraction arrives — survive until then.

Z-13: The Quarantine — Lore

Year 2039.
Humanity thought it had cured death. The Z-13 Virus was created in the quarantined city of Arkos — a once-bustling metropolis turned research colony. Scientists promised a serum that could regrow tissue, reverse aging, even resurrect the recently deceased.

But something went wrong. The test subjects came back — not alive, not dead — driven only by hunger. The virus mutated faster than anyone predicted. Within 72 hours, Arkos was sealed off from the world under Operation Black Dawn.

You are Joe Carter, a 24-year-old security contractor stationed on the perimeter. The last radio transmission from inside Arkos was a single phrase: “Containment breached. Zone 13 compromised.”

With the city in ruins, survivors scattered, and military rescue called off, you are trapped inside Quarantine Zone 13, armed with whatever you can scavenge. Every night new waves emerge from the fog — faster, stronger, hungrier. The infection evolves. The city whispers, and sometimes, it whispers your name.

Your mission: survive, discover what Z-13 really is, and reach extraction. But truth is contagious — and the cure may be worse than the disease.
